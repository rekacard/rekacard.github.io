Stage 1: Firestart the event with key information

  • What is the event theme?

  • Who are the key beneficiaries?

  • When will the event be held?

  • Secured a venue for the event?

Stage 2: Gather your team and start planning

  • Who are your event organizers?

  • Have you submitted budget application for approval?

  • Have you secured availability of beneficiary organization?

  • What will the event programme flow be?

Stage 3: Recruitment and grow your volunteers

  • How many volunteers will you need?

  • What key roles will you need to fill (e.g., photographer, emcee, logistics)?

  • Launch your registration form and monitor volunteer signup

  • Nurture volunteers who signed up by providing task lead opportunities.

Stage 4: Execute the event

  • Upload event brief for volunteers to read prior to event

  • Update volunteer attendance 'live'

  • Volunteer information (e.g., emergency contact, dietary requirements) at your finger tips

  • Gather immediate volunteer feedback at the end of the event

Stage 5: Event closure

  • Team debrief on challenges and things done well

  • Provide volunteer ratings to improve future event planning

  • Share event photos onto social media

  • Send out volunteer appreciation "Thank you" messages
